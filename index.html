The Narrative

The Story

Imagine walking into an Indian home in 1850.

Now imagine walking into one in 1920.

The furniture may be different. The politics may be different. The empire may be different.

But the most significant change is hanging quietly on the wall.

A picture.

A picture that millions of people have never questioned. A picture so familiar that it feels as though it has always been there.

And yet, barely a generation earlier, it did not exist.

Not in that form. Not in those colours. Not with that face.

For thousands of years, stories travelled by memory. Through songs. Performances. Manuscripts. Sculptures. Murals. And the voices of people who carried them forward.

Then something changed.

Images began travelling farther than ever before. And millions began sharing a common visual imagination.

How did this happen?

That is the story we will explore.

About

Cobalt Blue Foundation

Cobalt Blue Foundation is a multidisciplinary cultural organisation that brings together mythology, art, heritage, expressive arts, and human connection. Through lectures, workshops, and immersive programmes, we create spaces where ancient stories meet contemporary minds.

Our work explores the deep connections between visual culture, collective memory, and individual identity — asking the questions that the familiar never invites us to ask.

We believe that understanding the stories behind our stories is the first step toward understanding ourselves.

Mission
Making India's cultural inheritance intellectually accessible to every generation
Vision
A world that remembers where its imagination came from
Impact
